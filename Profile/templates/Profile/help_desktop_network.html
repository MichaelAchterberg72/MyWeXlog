Desktop Network - Help

 

This is a summary of the people that have connected to Wexlog that are part of your network.

Your network is the people that have connected to and have become members of the WeXlog services. These Network members are members that have connected as a result of a validation request invite or have been specifically invited by you to join the WeXlog family.

A networked member is recognised as a result of your direct invite request. Members that are presubscribed members that you request validations from will not be included in your network.

The Levels explanation:

  • Level 1 - Members that have joined as a direct result of your direct invite requests
  • Level 2 - Members that join as a result of the Level 1 member invite requests
  • Level 3 - Members that join as a result of the Level 2 member invite requests
  • Level 4 - Members that join as a result of the Level 3 member invite requests
  • Level 5 - Members that join as a result of the Level 4 member invite requests
