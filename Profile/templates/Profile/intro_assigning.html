Assigning a Vacancy

As the Employer, once the interview process has been completed you need to navigate to the ‘Vacancy Dashboard’ page and select from the ‘Suitable Applicants’ which one you wish to assign the vacancy to by clicking on ‘Assign’.

This will open a page that allows you to capture the currency Rate offered as well as the anticipated start and end dates required.
