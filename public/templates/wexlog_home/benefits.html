Benefits

The hardest part of being a freelancer, a consultant or a contractor is finding the work. In many cases, the cost of this can be 30% or even more of the value of a contract! The alternative is to use a broker or agent to do the search, which also comes at a cost. And the results are still largely the result of chance, of making the enquiry at the time the vacancy is open.

WeXlog allows you to take a large part of the cost and chance out of the process, by allowing you to post your qualifications in a place where you will be found by the people who search for those educational qualifications, experience and skills at the time they do the search. You are no longer dependent on good luck, the memory of the searcher or the agents contacts with the people who may want you. Best of all, you are able to include the date on which you will be available for a project, so the searchers can look for you for future work.

Capturing your skills and experiences as they are put to use ensures that the record is fresh, accurate and exhaustive. It avoids the need to spend hours re-evaluating the daily events of a project after months of work have dimmed your memory of the details of what you did. That shows your professionalism, and enhances your standing in the eyes of potential clients, as well as ensuring that every aspect of your work history is available to a potential client who might be looking for an element of it that would not normally stand out. Your work normally consists of many small details, and WeXlog enables each of them to be recorded individually, and to stand out when a client searches for a particular one.

A great advantage of WeXlog is that it enables you to update your record on a daily, weekly or monthly basis with very little effort – and it can take as little as a minute to do it! It ensures that your record remains accurate and up to date, and always ready to attract the attention of a client you might easily have missed. Even if you are ready to attract the next client only a year or two later, the detail is still fresh and accurate, and the simultaneous verification of it makes your record both comprehensive and professional.

Confirmed Experience

Have you had the experience of finding a candidate who appears, on the surface, to offer the experience you need, only to find that the experience claimed is largely in his or her mind?

The problem is exacerbated by the fact that the institutional memory of a particular employee or contractor is generally limited or even non-existent, and some companies even have a policy of not giving testimonials to departing employees or contractors that set out more than simply the fact and dates of their employment.

WeXlog prevents that, by giving members the ability to log their experience, together with evaluations by named and responsible supervisors or colleagues while they are still fresh in their minds, and to make that available to you at the click of your mouse. You avoid the delay and expense of confirming the claimed experience, and you are able to track the development and performance over years!

Trust Passport

Create your trust passport of qualified references.
WeXlog enables employers and contractees to record the details of the requirements and their performance in it at the time it takes place. It solves the problem of needing to track down the responsible manager in respect of work that was performed months or even years before, a problem that has become more significant in recent years, with an increase in the rate of change of management at all levels of organizations, which often makes it impossible to obtain a subsequent certification of more than the simple fact of that employment. WeXlog ensures that the recording of the job and performance in it is made at the time it happens!
