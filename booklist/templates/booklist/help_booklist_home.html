Booklist Home Page - Help

This page displayes a list of the books that you have captured as having read.

The books are listed in order of having been read and displayed with the book name (publisher), the book type and the date read. This information is displayed in your public profile under the heading of Books Read. The profile will also list the Total Books Read: displaying them by Title, Publisher, Author(s) Format andDate Completed. If you have not captured any books it will state "No books listed".

Should you wish to see a listing of all the books that have been captured members into the WeXlog system click on the 'View All Book' button.
