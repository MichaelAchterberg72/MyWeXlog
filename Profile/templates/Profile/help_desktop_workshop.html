Desktop Workshop - Help

 

This is a summary of the assignments that you have as an Employer or as a Talent.

The summary displays the active open assignments.

As Employer
The enter office button will open a page displaying Open Assignments, Pending Assignments, Completed Assignments.
As Talent
The enter workshop button will opens the assignments page as required by the Talent detailing Open Assignments, Pending Assignments, Completed Assignments and Declined Assignments.


As Employer

Assignments are created in interview process in which once the interview has been completed you may assign the vacancy to a talent by utilising the 'Assign' button.

Once the vacancy has been assigned it will be listed as an open assignment under the 'As Employer' heading.


As Talent

Assignments are created after the interview has been completed and the employer assigns the vacancy to a specific talent.
