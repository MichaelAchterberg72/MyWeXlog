Create an Online Trust Passport

This is a simple hero unit, a simple jumbotron-style component for calling extra attention to featured content or information.

WexLog provides a convenient and reliable way to obtain and record daily, weekly, monthly or project activities, with a real-time recording of the evaluation by your employer, client or customer. It is no longer necessary to obtain a written testimonial. It is no longer necessary to locate a supervisor who has left the company, a client who has moved to another start-up business, or a customer who has bought a new home. WexLog records and keeps your work and skills history as they happen, and makes them available when you need them.

WexLog is a perfect way to facilitate and encourage repeat business from a satisfied client or customer, or an offer from a past employer who might have lost track of your progress. As a good performer, you no longer fade into background memory or, even worse, into an outdated memory of what you were back then. You remain a trusted, identifiable and easily fond resource, with known and updated skills and experience.
