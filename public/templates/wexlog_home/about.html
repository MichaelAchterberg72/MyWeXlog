Who We Are

Our Mission The Problem The Solution

Affordable Experience Tracking with Quality Vacancies

WeXlog's mission is to create a portal that is aimed at allowing people to capture their experience and have it ratified or signed off by their clients / superiors / peers whilst providing a portal for vacancies.

WeXlog is designed to allow people who have been working for several years and have a large amount of experience that may not be able to be authorised as they no longer work with those clients or at the company any longer. The portal allows people to capture this time, however this is displayed as such.

The captured time will then be saved against their profile, available for people to search for that experience.

Once captured, clients requiring skills or services should perform a search and find people with the needed experience. The results would be displayed in terms of distance from the prospective client, experience and ratings.

The Problem

The working world is moving toward a gig economy at an increasing rate. In this economy, people offer their skills on a freelance basis, marketing their abilities through their networks. There are methods through which people can market their abilities available, however these methods are static and do not give an indication as to the level of experience a person has apart from the length of their working career.

These methods are also inconsistent in that a person that has over 10 years overall experience, in construction for example, but has only been involved in airport construction for 1 year does not easily get noticed.

Similarly, in the service industry, people do not know what experience technicians/mechanics etc. have when it comes to their particular needs. If you had an Audi diesel, you would want to identify the mechanic that has the most experience with that car as well as whether they offer a quality service.

In the service industry it is possible to rate a person’s service using platforms currently available, however, that rating may be made by a person that does not know your car. Indeed, the mechanic in question may be an expert on another model but know very little of yours.

In the professional services industry one can source skills from a global market, but the question remains: How do you find people with ratified experience in the field you require.

The concept of a timesheet is familiar with many people in the legal, engineering, accounting, and many other fields. But this system is designed to measure the amount of work performed for billing purposes.

By extending this system, one can add the knowledge and skill used in performing that work and build up a knowledge base which is approved by a client or superior, not simply added in an uncontrolled fashion.

Capture Education & Experience

Your actual work experience, the ability to apply your skills and experience, as well as your value to clients, customers, employers and team members are of vital value to such persons in the future, but are difficult to accumulate and record.

WeXlog gives you that ability in an easily-verifiable and trustworthy way. Each project, job and work position can be recorded and verified in real-time, and be retained for the future in a structure that is easily searchable by clients, customers and employers who need a precise set of skills, experience, training and education, using a sophisticated and easy to use search function. Even the most unusual and esoteric skills and knowledge are catered for. If what you offer is not listed, you can add a descriptor, which creates a prompt for future searchers to enable them to find you.

Your entries can be verified by the client, customer or employer in real-time, providing a living CV, which you can make available to searchers.

Post Your Availability

The core objective of WeXlog is to facilitate the recording of skills and achievements that you generate in the course of your daily activities and make them available on a platform that markets those skills to suitable bids and positions. By utilising WexLog these skills and achievements with referrals and recomendations from your collegues, supervisors and clients enable you to market yourself and your living CV for vacancies on which you are able to bid on as well as for positions that become available.

You are providing a platform to bring your capabilities to the attention of customers, clients and employers. WeXlog allows you to engage and demonstrate continuous learning, ongoing beneficial activities and internal communication of those skills whilst in your day to day actions at work.

Business activities that you practice daily and apply to the development of your career through the use of technology allow you to work faster and smater in every field, using the facility WeXlog allows you to market and develop those skills in the development of your career.

In order to market your skills and living CV you need to capture, on a weekly or fortnightly basis, your activities into the WeXlog portal through the use of the 'Add Experience' or 'Add Education' forms and then post your availability.

This will automatically generate a listing of 'Vacancies' or positions that you will be able to 'Bid' on.

Post a Vacancy or Search

Finding the right consultant, contractor or freelancer can be a nightmare, requiring extensive investigation and checking. When the job is completed, you sit back and relax, to prepare for the next round, often needing to undergo the whole process again, because the best of those available are committed on other projects, or you have lost track of them and have to start the process from the beginning again after a year or three. WeXlog avoids that nightmare.

With WeXlog you can post your short term positions for consultants, contractors and freelancers as well as for permanent staff positions and have the members that are available place their bids on the vacancies posted. All you need to do is companre the bids received and evaluate which one suits your budget, your skill set requirements and cultural fit with the motivation that is provided on every bid.

WeXlog sets out for you a good selection of the people with the education, skills and experience you need, each one with a verified CV and a present and future availability, all up to the minute, so that you can make a good, informed choice of candidates to shortlist or even simply approach.
