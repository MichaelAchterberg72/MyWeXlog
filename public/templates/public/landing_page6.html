Benefits

 

Company Benefits

Find Resources

Find the skills, companies and resources that fit your requirements.
The needs of companies change rapidly in the modern world and the ability to find people with those skills is often difficult. In addition, one never knows if the skills claimed are indeed correct.
WexLog solves these problems by offering a single source to find those people, each with a verified work history.

International Portfolio of skills

The listing of members and their skills is internationally recognised across a wide portfolio of skills and industries. This is reinforced by the recording of the work or contract history and the real-time evaluation of performance by the responsible persons, making WexLog a simple, reliable and important resource for marketing and searching for skilled people.

Productised Resources

A company often doesn’t need a full time resource, but nonetheless needs the skill. With WexLog, the company can advertise for the amount of time they need the skill and pay for that time only. In this way a company has access to a much broader field of skills which they can pay for as needed.

Confirmed Experience

Be able to see how much experience someone has, confirmed by third parties.
Often, one of the most difficult and time-consuming parts of the recruitment process is the need to verify the claims of performance and satisfaction made by candidates. This is exacerbated by the fact that the institutional memory of the performance of a particular person, whether good, bad or simply indifferent, fades rapidly over even a short time. WexLog avoids this problem by permitting job requirements and performance to be recorded contemporaneously. This ensures that the actual activity of the candidate is recorded at the time it takes place, with the person responsible for managing that activity certifying the requirements and their performance while the activity is still fresh in their minds.

Trust Passport

Create your trust passport of qualified references.
WexLog enables employers and contractees to record the details of the requirements and their performance in it at the time it takes place. It solves the problem of needing to track down the responsible manager in respect of work that was performed months or even years before, a problem that has become more significant in recent years, with an increase in the rate of change of management at all levels of organizations, which often makes it impossible to obtain a subsequent certification of more than the simple fact of that employment. WexLog ensures that the recording of the job and performance in it is made at the time it happens!

 

Personal Benefits

Advertise Experience

WexLog offers members the opportunity to place their work activities, skills and qualifications, on a searchable database all in a place trusted by recruiters and those looking for difficult to find skills and attributes. Best of all, the fact that the claims made by members are audited and certified by a third party, giving the searchers the assurance that what they find is what they will get, without the tedious process of checking, of verifying claims of performance made years ago.
How often have you been forced to explain to an enquirer that the work was done by a manager who is now retired, or has moved to another company or another country? WexLog makes that a thing of the past! How often have you seen an ideal job advertised, with the closing date for application ten days ago? That is no longer a problem with a WexLog membership!

Enable freelancers and consultants to advertise their experience

Freelancers and consultants know that the hardest, most time-consuming part of their work is finding a job in the first place. WexLog makes it possible to have a permanent advertisement of your skills, education, qualifications and experience in a single place, trusted by potential employers for its accurate, up to date and certified records.

WexLog allows people to productise their skills to several different companies and in that way be able to freelance or work on retainer, giving them a lot of flexibility.

Productise Experience

Enable you to productise your skills and advertise to market.
How do you bring to the attention of possible employers the unique and hard to find skills and experience you have? WexLog gives you the opportunity to show the world that you have those skills and experience, and to set out for possible users what they may mean to them in terms of innovation, efficiency and enhanced competitiveness, all in an on-line searchable database.

View Skills Internationally

View skills available and required internationally. Skills and experience are increasingly becoming internationally relevant, but the process of finding them has not become easier. Now, WexLog offers members the opportunity to set out their skills and experience to the world, while allowing those looking for those skills and experience to search for them internationally, simply by the click of a mouse.

Search & Apply

Search for and apply for positions based on your skills base. WexLog offers an opportunity to people and employers to advertise their needs for skills and experience in a record available to all members. There is no longer a need to search dozens of employment agencies and classified ads. Simply use WexLog to search positions and contracts available, using the keywords that describe what you can offer, and refer the advertiser to your datafile by a click of the mouse, with the possibility to enhance and personalize your communication.
