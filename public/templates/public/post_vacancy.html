Post a Vacancy

Post a vacancy and have the bids come to you.

Finding the right consultant, contractor or freelancer can be a nightmare, requiring extensive investigation and checking. When the job is completed, you sit back and relax, to prepare for the next round, often needing to undergo the whole process again, because the best of those available are committed on other projects, or you have lost track of them and have to start the process from the beginning again after a year or three. WexLog avoids that nightmare.

With WexLog you can post your short term positions for consultants, contractors and freelancers as well as for permanent staff positions and have the members that are available place their bids on the vacancies posted. All you need to do is companre the bids received and evaluate which one suits your budget, your skill set requirements and cultural fit with the motivation that is provided on every bid.

WexLog sets out for you a good selection of the people with the education, skills and experience you need, each one with a verified CV and a present and future availability, all up to the minute, so that you can make a good, informed choice of candidates to shortlist or even simply approach.
