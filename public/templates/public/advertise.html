Advertise Personal Skills, Experience and Availability

Marketing of your skills is a central core activity of the WexLog platform.

The core objective of WexLog is to facilitate the recording of skills and achievements that you generate in the course of your daily activities and make them available on a platform that markets those skills to suitable bids and positions. By utilising WexLog these skills and achievements with referrals and recomendations from your collegues. supervisors and clients enable you to market yourself and your living CV for vacancies on which you are able to bid on as well as for positions that become available.

You are providing a platform to bring your capabilities to the attention of customers, clients and employers. Wexlog allows you to engage and demonstrate continuous learning, ongoing beneficial activities and internal communication of those skills whilst in your day to day actions at work.

Business activities that you practice daily and apply to the development of your career through the use of technology allow you to work faster and smater in every field, using the facility WexLog provides allows you to market and develop those skills in the development of your career.

In order to market your skills and living CV you need to capture, on a weekly or fortnightly basis, your activities into the WexLog portal through the use of the 'Add Experience' or 'Add Education' forms.

This will automatically generate a listing of 'Vacancies' or positions that you will be able to 'Bid' on.
