This is broken down into two sections, the number of Experience confirmations you have been requested and the Interviews Pending.


Experience confirmations

This will list validation requests that have been requested in the event that you are a lecturer, classmate, colleague, supervisor or manager, worked with someone, as well as if someone has worked with your company as a consultant/contractor.

The options you will be provided with when validating experience will be:

Allows you to comfirm the experience without providing a comment.
Allows you to reject the validation request, reasons are personal.
Review with Comment Preferred option which allows you to confirm the experience and provide a comment as to the performance of the validation requestee. This option is usually utilised by managers or clients.
In the event that the requestee has got the wrong email address resulting in a validation request from the wrong person.


Interviews Pending

This details the number of interviews that have been requested in the form of an interview request from a potential employer as well as on the side of the talent who submitted the proposal bid for any vacancies advertised.
