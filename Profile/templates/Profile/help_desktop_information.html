Desktop Information - Help

 

The desktop Information dialog box lists icons which allow you access to the summary profile information.

The fields required are:

Opens a copy of your Public Profile
Opens a copy of your Summary for Industry, Project and Designation.
Opens a copy of your settings

Public Profile

The public profile is what potential employers see when they view your information relating to a vacancy and the skills that match it.

This is also the information that will be make available should you publish your public profile link for additional information on your CV, LinkedIn profile, Facebook profile, etc.


Summary for Industry, Project and Designation

This lists a Designation Summary, a Company Summary detailing the amount of hours you have captured whilst capturing your skills that you have worked at a company and branch as well as a Project summary detailing a list of the time spent on a Project.


Settings

This lists the features you are able to on and off within your MyWeXlog environment.
