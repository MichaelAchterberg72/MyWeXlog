Benefits

 

Company Benefits

WexLog allows you to set out your company for the world to see. If they don’t know who you are, what you can do and what you offer, they may not know about you. WexLog is the right place to set out the information potential customers and star employees need to fit you into their plans:

  • Your plant, machinery and work capabilities
  • Your skills
  • Your experience

Best of all, your entry is always there when customers look for those assets, and they can search for you with the click of a mouse, without a need to search through their files for the brochures they picked up at a Trade Show, if they remember who to look for! WexLog allows your company to be always front of mind when a customer has a need, or when a good potential employee is considering his next career move. Can you afford to settle for obscurity?

Whether you are a large corporation or a small business you need to hire the right person to do the job. The needs of companies change rapidly in the modern world. They become more specialized and more complex. The search to find people to meet those needs is often difficult, and it is made more complex by the need to determine whether the claims made by those people are justified.

WexLog solves those problems by offering a single source to find those people, each with a trust passport verified work history. If you don’t immediately find the particular skills-set you are seeking, WexLog makes it easy to post your requirement in a place where qualified candidates will find it, and then respond to you.

WexLog’s international listing of skills is widely recognized and trusted. It embraces an extensive portfolio of skills and industries. This is ensured by a contemporaneous recording of work performance and project history, and the real-time evaluation of performance by a responsible third-party supervisor or a qualified colleague makes WexLog a simple to use, reliable and trustworthy resource for marketing your skills or seeking qualified employees, contractors or skills suppliers

A company often does not need a full-time resource, but nonetheless needs the skill for a period or regular periods. This is an increasing feature of the modern world, but, unfortunately, it is often disproportionately difficult and expensive to find the one person who will satisfy your needs. No longer! Wexlog allows you to search an extensive list of qualified and verified candidates, and to choose your ideal one. You can choose the skill, the education, the experience and the level of advancement you want.

Have you had the experience of finding a candidate who appears, on the surface, to offer the experience you need, only to find that the experience claimed is largely in his or her mind?

The problem is exacerbated by the fact that the institutional memory of a particular employee or contractor is generally limited or even non-existent, and some companies even have a policy of not giving testimonials to departing employees or contractors that set out more than simply the fact and dates of their employment.

WexLog prevents that, by giving members the ability to log their experience, together with evaluations by named and responsible supervisors or colleagues while they are still fresh in their minds, and to make that available to you at the click of your mouse. You avoid the delay and expense of confirming the claimed experience, and you are able to track the development and performance over years!

Create your trust passport of qualified references.
WexLog enables employers and contractees to record the details of the requirements and their performance in it at the time it takes place. It solves the problem of needing to track down the responsible manager in respect of work that was performed months or even years before, a problem that has become more significant in recent years, with an increase in the rate of change of management at all levels of organizations, which often makes it impossible to obtain a subsequent certification of more than the simple fact of that employment. WexLog ensures that the recording of the job and performance in it is made at the time it happens!

 

Personal Benefits

WexLog offers members the opportunity to place their work activities, skills and qualifications, on a searchable database all in a place trusted by recruiters and those looking for difficult to find skills and attributes. Best of all, the fact that the claims made by members are audited and certified by a third party, giving the searchers the assurance that what they find is what they will get, without the tedious process of checking, or verifying claims of performance made years ago.
How often have you been forced to explain to an enquirer that the work was done by a manager who is now retired, or has moved to another company or another country? WexLog makes that a thing of the past! How often have you seen an ideal job advertised, with the closing date for application ten days ago? That is no longer a problem with a WexLog membership!

Often, the hardest, most expensive and most time-consuming part of the Freelancer and Consultant’s lives is the process of finding the work! WexLog allows you to place a standing advertisement of what you can offer, making it available to a targeted search by a qualified employer, and the fact that WexLog allows you to accumulate the work you have done and the confirmation of its quality ensures that the potential employers who find you already know that your work is of the required quality. Best of all, when you have done work for a company in the past, WexLog ensures that you remain front-of-mind, even after a period of years.

How do you bring to potential users the skills and experience you have? How do you display your innovative capabilities, your attention to detail, your high level of productivity to potential clients and employers? How do you put yourself in the world market?

WexLog enables you to put on record your unique set of skills, education, experience and capabilities, the qualities that make you stand out in a crowd, to offer them to the world, whether across the city or across the world. It gives you freedom of choice!

View skills available and required internationally. Skills and experience are increasingly becoming internationally relevant, but the process of finding them has not become easier. Now, WexLog offers members the opportunity to set out their skills and experience to the world, while allowing those looking for those skills and experience to search for them internationally, simply by the click of a mouse.

WexLog members are able to conduct a simple search for positions or contracts that match what they are seeking and are able to offer. When the choice has been made, the application process is almost as simple as clicking your mouse.

The process of entering experience, education and skills into WexLog as they are gained, ensures that the recruiter knows precisely what he or she is getting. There is no need to undergo the tedious process of checking the detail of references and claims provided by the other side. What you see is what you get! That is why WexLog is known as a trusted source for recruitment of staff, contractors and consultants, each one with a documented and verified history.
