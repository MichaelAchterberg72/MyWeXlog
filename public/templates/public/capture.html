Capture and Verify Experience

This is a simple hero unit, a simple jumbotron-style component for calling extra attention to featured content or information.

Your actual work experience, the ability to apply your skills and experience, as well as your value to clients, customers, employers and team members are of vital value to such persons in the future, but are difficult to accumulate and record.

WexLog gives you that ability in an easily-verifiable and trustworthy way. Each project, job and work position can be recorded and verified in real-time, and be retained for the future in a structure that is easily searchable by clients, customers and employers who need a precise set of skills, experience, training and education, using a sophisticated and easy to use search function. Even the most unusual and esoteric skills and knowledge are catered for. If what you offer is not listed, you can add a descriptor, which creates a prompt for future searchers to enable them to find you.

Your entries can be verified by the client, customer or employer in real-time, providing a living CV, which you can make available to searchers.

Because the service offered by WexLog is internet-based you record your capabilities on an international platform, enabling searchers to find you wherever you – and they – may be. To make it even more convenient, your record can also be verified internationally, quickly and easily.

The range of skills you can record is unlimited; one WexLog member is a lawyer, a management consultant skilled in profit improvement, new unit handholding and employment creation, a fiction novelist, a blogger, and a copywriter, as well as a financial mathematician and a long-distance sailor, as well as being experienced in fifteen countries. All of these attributes and qualities are separately and jointly searchable in one place.
